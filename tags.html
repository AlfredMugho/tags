About Me

I was born in Atlanta Geogia on February 24th 1998 to my parents Dufton and Roselyn Mugho. Both my parents are Kenyan, making me a first generation African American. With that, I have great responsibility not only to attain my full potential and ensure that my parent's dream become a reality, but to also ensure that I take full advantage to all the resources and opportunities I am privilaged to have here at the University of Washington.

Throughout my life, I have lived in three countries and experienced many different cultures and peoples. I always hope to share my experiences, and seek to be as pluralistic and as open minded as possible. In the modern world this is essential, because today, our socity is more diverse than ever before!
